Who We Are

Trailer Company was originally founded to provide trailers to local sailors for travel to regattas and cruising locales. In the following years the company has grown to accomodate the needs of an ever increasing community of sailors from Alaska to Florida, from Newfoundland to Australia.

For ten years we have built custom sailboat trailers, one at a time. Our focus is on quality and funcionality. Since we own and sail a variety of boats, we have a good idea of what works and what doesn't. We will be happy to consult with you and offer ideas and pricing prior to your purchase, and we will offer you our competitors' contact information if you like, confident that once you compare product, price and knowledge, you will return..

We are active members of the Redacted Sailing Club and try to get our boats to the ocean a couple times per year.
